Here are some safety guidelines to follow during a duathlon event:

  1. Study the course map and become familiar with any potential hazards, such as sharp turns, steep descents, or uneven surfaces.
  2. Warm up your muscles with light exercises and stretching before the event to reduce the risk of injury.
  3. Put on a properly fitting helmet that meets safety standards.
  4. Maintain control of your speed and movements at all times, especially when descending or passing other participants. Avoid sudden movements that could result in collisions or falls.
  5. If you or another participant has a medical emergency or injury during the event, seek medical attention immediately from our event staff or medical personnel.
